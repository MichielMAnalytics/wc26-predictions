## How to use / caveats

- Scores are tuned to **maximise Scorito expected points**, not to look realistic: many 1-0/0-1 picks are correct because the outcome (toto, 30 pts) dominates and a minimal-margin favourite win is the single most-likely exact score.

- Backtest skill (see MODEL.md): ~26% RPS improvement over climatology, 60% outcome accuracy out-of-sample; tournaments are high-variance so treat deep-run picks as edges, not certainties.

- Confirm Scorito's exact point values in-app before submitting; penalty shootouts don't count toward scores.
